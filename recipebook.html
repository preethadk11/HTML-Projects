2 minutes Maggi

Maggi is the easiest and quickest recipe anyone can do and anyone me even a 10 old girl/boy.You don't have to spend hours to do this delicious thing.It adds a little twist of spices and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles
  • 1 packet Maggi tastemaker
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a pan
  2. Add chopped onions and saute until they turn slightly golder
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft
  4. Add 1 cup of water and let it boil
  5. Break the Maggi noodles into the water
  6. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes on medium flame
  7. Cook until the noodles becmoe soft and absorb the water
  8. Turn off the heat and serve hot!
